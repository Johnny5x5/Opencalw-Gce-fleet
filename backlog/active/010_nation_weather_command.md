# Backlog Item: Weather Command (Science Ministry)

**Status:** Pending
**Priority:** High (Public Service)
**Department:** SDN Weather Command

## Objective
Establish a sovereign source of meteorological truth and scientific research.

## Requirements
1.  **Data Ingestion:**
    -   Integrate **OpenWeatherMap API** (Cost-effective).
    -   Integrate **NOAA / NWS API** (Free, US-centric).
    -   Integrate **European ECMWF** data where possible.

2.  **Public Broadcasting:**
    -   Build a "Weather Report" bot that posts daily forecasts to the Social Fabric and Discord.
    -   Focus on "Actionable Intelligence" (e.g., "Severe Storm Warning: Secure Data Centers in Virginia").

3.  **Science Division:**
    -   Use aggregated data to train custom ML models for hyper-local prediction.
    -   Publish research papers (generated by AI Scientists) on climate trends.
