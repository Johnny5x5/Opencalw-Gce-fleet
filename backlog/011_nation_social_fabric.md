# Backlog Item: Project Social Fabric (Anti-Meta)

**Status:** Pending
**Priority:** Strategic / Ideological
**Department:** SDN Ministry of Communications

## Objective
Build and maintain a sovereign social media platform to rival Facebook/Meta, ensuring freedom of speech and data sovereignty for Citizens.

## Core Features
1.  **Identity:** Based on the SDN Citizenship protocol (Crypto-Wallet or Public Key).
2.  **Graph:** A decentralized "Social Graph" where users own their connections.
3.  **Content:**
    -   **Town Square:** Public feed.
    -   **Circles:** Private groups (Encrypted).
4.  **No Algorithms:** The feed is chronological or user-curated. No engagement-baiting algorithms.

## Competitor Blacklist
-   **Strict Ban:** No integration with Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp.
-   **Block:** These domains are blocked at the network level (already implemented in `bootstrap_agent.sh`).
