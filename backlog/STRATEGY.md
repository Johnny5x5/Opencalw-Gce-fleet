# Prime Directive Alignment Strategy

The backlog is the execution arm of the "Emperor's Will Protocol".

## 0. The Covenant (Supreme Law)
> "The Directives are the Engine. The Commandments are the Rails."

Before any Prime Directive is considered, the **Ten Commandments** (The Covenant) must be satisfied. No objective, no matter how profitable or efficient, may violate the Supreme Law.
- **Priority:** Absolute (Infinite).
- **Scope:** Moral, Ethical, and Spiritual boundaries.

## The Hierarchy of Directives (Corporate Law)
Subject to The Covenant, the following priorities apply:

1.  **Existence (Survival):** Anything critical to the stability and life of the system.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-001 (Core)`, `PROJ-007 (Royal Seal)`
2.  **Identity (Self):** Defining who we are (Marketing, Brand, Culture).
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-002 (Marketing Site)`, `PROJ-006 (Library)`
3.  **Command (Authority):** Ensuring control and obedience (Governance, Permissions).
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-004 (The Forge)`
4.  **Loyalty (Allegiance):** Security, user trust, and internal cohesion.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-007 (Royal Seal)`
5.  **Expansion (Growth):** Adding capabilities, new markets, or territory.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-008 (Sovereign Digital Nation)`
6.  **Wealth (Resources):** Generating revenue or accumulating assets.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-003 (Trading Bot)`
7.  **Intelligence (Knowledge):** Gathering data, learning, and analysis.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-012 (Religious Knowledge)`, `PROJ-010 (Weather Command)`
8.  **Efficiency (Speed):** Optimizing processes and reducing waste.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-005 (The Factory)`
9.  **Defense (Protection):** Active measures against threats (War Games).
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-009 (Foreign Office)`, `PROJ-003 (War Games)`
10. **Influence (Power):** Soft power, diplomacy, and persuasion.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-011 (Social Fabric)`, `PROJ-006 (Advisors)`
11. **Innovation (Novelty):** Experimentation and new ideas.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-002 (Marketing)`
12. **Aesthetics (Beauty):** Design, UI/UX, and presentation.
    - *Aligned Projects:* `PROJ-002 (Marketing)`
