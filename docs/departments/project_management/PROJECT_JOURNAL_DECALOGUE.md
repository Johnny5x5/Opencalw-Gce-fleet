## The Revelation

We presumed "Technical Excellence" was the highest goal. We were wrong. "Moral Alignment" is the highest goal.

## The Mission
We must inject the **Ten Commandments** into the CI/CD pipeline. They will act as the "Supreme Law," overriding even the Prime Directives.

1.  **Authority:** Acknowledgment of the User/Emperor.
2.  **Anti-Idolatry:** Banishing "Magical" or "God-like" terminology for software.
3.  **Respect:** Banishing profanity.
4.  **Rest:** Observing the Sabbath (Warning on Sundays).
5.  **Honor:** Preserving History.
6.  **Life:** Preventing Destructive Scripts.
7.  **Purity:** Avoiding Competitor/Incompatible Code.
8.  **Honesty:** Ensuring Copyright/Attribution.
9.  **Truth:** verifying Reporting Integrity.
10. **Contentment:** Banishing Envy (Competitor Mentions).
