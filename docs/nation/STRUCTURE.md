# Structure of the Sovereign Digital Nation

## 1. The Foreign Office (State Department)
*   **Mission:** Manage relations with External Powers (UN, NATO, 195 Nations).
*   **Capabilities:**
    *   **The Mirror:** Real-time digital twins of all major governments.
    *   **Diplomatic Corps:** Agents assigned to monitor and engage with specific nations.
    *   **Intelligence:** Gathering political and geopolitical data.

## 2. Weather Command (Science Ministry)
*   **Mission:** Provide truthful, scientific data to the Citizens and the World.
*   **Capabilities:**
    *   **Global Sensor Grid:** Aggregation of API data (OpenWeather, NOAA).
    *   **Forecast:** Predictive modeling.
    *   **Research:** Climate science and atmospheric study.

## 3. The Social Fabric (Ministry of Communications)
*   **Mission:** Provide a sovereign communication platform free from competitor influence (Meta/X).
*   **Capabilities:**
    *   **Town Square:** Public forums.
    *   **Identity:** Verifiable digital citizenship.
    *   **News:** State-verified reporting.

## 4. The Treasury (Ministry of Finance)
*   **Note:** Distinct from the Conglomerate's Finance Department.
*   **Mission:** Manage the Nation's reserves (Crypto/Gold) and taxes collected from the Business via Treaty.
