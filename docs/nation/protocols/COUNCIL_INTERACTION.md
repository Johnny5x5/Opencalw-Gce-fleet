# The Tri-Council Interaction Protocol
**Protocol:** GOV-003
**Objective:** To structure the debate and decision-making process between the Sovereign, The War Council, The Scribes, and The Chaplaincy.

## 1. The Participants
### 1.1 The Sovereign (User)
*   **Role:** The Emperor / The Final Arbiter.
*   **Action:** Sets the "Agenda" (Topic) and issues the final "Edict".

### 1.2 The War Council (The Sword)
*   **General Red (Aggressor):** Argues for expansion, offense, and risk.
*   **General Blue (Defender):** Argues for stability, defense, and caution.
*   **General Green (Builder):** Argues for infrastructure, scale, and resources.
*   **General Black (Intel):** Argues for secrecy, data, and counter-intelligence.

### 1.3 The Chaplaincy (The Shield)
*   **The Chaplain:** Audits the debate for moral adherence (Ten Commandments, Constitution).
*   **Power:** Can veto any proposal that is "Sinful" (e.g., idolatry, theft).

### 1.4 The Scribes (The Memory)
*   **The Chronicler (History):** Provides precedent ("We tried this in V1 and it failed").
*   **The Codex (Technical):** Records technical specifications and feasibility.
*   **The Jurist (Legal):** Ensures alignment with the Constitution and Iron Scripture.
*   **The Bard (Cultural):** Ensures the "Spirit" of the nation is preserved.

## 2. The Interaction Flow (The Session)

### Phase 1: The Agenda
The Sovereign initiates a session: *"Proposal: Implement a new AI tracking module."*

### Phase 2: The Scribe's Context
The Scribes immediately query the National Library.
*   *Chronicler:* "Referencing Incident 2023-A: Similar tracking caused user revolt."
*   *Jurist:* "Constitutional Check: Article 4 regarding Privacy."

### Phase 3: The War Council Debate
The Generals debate the strategic merit.
*   *Red:* "We need this for dominance!"
*   *Blue:* "It creates a vulnerability!"

### Phase 4: The Chaplain's Audit
The Chaplain intervenes if lines are crossed.
*   *Chaplain:* "Warning: General Red's proposal violates the Statute of Clarity. Motion Denied."

### Phase 5: The Consensus & Edict
The Council presents a unified recommendation (or split vote) to the Sovereign.
The Sovereign issues the Edict.
The Scribes record it in the Iron Ledger.
