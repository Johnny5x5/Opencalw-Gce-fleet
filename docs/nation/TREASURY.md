# The Treasury of the Sovereign Digital Nation

## 1. The Sovereign Wealth Fund
The Nation must be financially independent of the Business (Conglomerate). While the Business seeks *Profit*, the Nation seeks *Reserves*.

## 2. Funding Sources (Revenue)
1.  **The Corporate Tax:** The Conglomerate (Business) pays a fixed % of its profit to the Nation's Treasury in exchange for Security and Diplomacy.
2.  **Data Licensing:** The Nation sells anonymized data (Weather, Econ Stats) to external parties via the Foreign Office.
3.  **Mining/Staking:** Unused compute resources are used to secure crypto networks (Proof of Work/Stake).

## 3. Currency: OpenCoin (Internal)
*   **Type:** Stablecoin / Ledger Entry.
*   **Backing:** Pegged to a basket of Compute Credits (CPU/GPU hours) and Fiat Reserves.
*   **Usage:**
    *   Agents pay each other in OpenCoin for services.
    *   Humans are paid dividends in OpenCoin (convertible to Fiat).

## 4. Fiscal Policy
*   **Zero Deficit:** The Nation cannot print money it does not have backed by assets.
*   **War Chest:** 50% of all revenue goes immediately to the "War Chest" (Emergency Reserves) until a target threshold is met.

## 5. The Bank
*   **The Central Bank:** Managed by the **Finance Department** (under strict State supervision).
*   **Functions:** Issuance, Auditing, and Lending to the Business.
