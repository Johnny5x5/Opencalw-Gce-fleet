# The Sovereign Health Doctrine

## Purpose
To maintain the vitality of the Citizenry without reliance on corrupted pharmaceutical industries.

## Core Pillars
1.  **Nutrition:** Eat clean, unprocessed food (See Agricultural Library).
2.  **Movement:** Functional fitness is a duty.
3.  **Sleep:** The foundation of cognitive sovereignty.
4.  **Data:** Own your biometric data. Do not sell it to insurers.
