# Title: The Sovereign Water Rights & Stewardship Act

## Section 1: Definition of Terms
1.  **Water Body:** Any river, stream, lake, aquifer, spring, or reservoir within the Sovereign Digital Nation.
2.  **Commercial Extraction:** The withdrawal of water for industrial processes, agricultural irrigation for profit, or bottling for sale.
3.  **Personal Use:** The withdrawal of water for drinking, cooking, hygiene, and personal gardening/livestock.
4.  **Jubilee Year:** Every seventh (7th) year in the Sovereign Calendar, designated as a year of aquifer recharge.
5.  **Grand Jubilee Year:** Every fiftieth (50th) year in the Sovereign Calendar.

## Section 2: The Jubilee Moratorium
1.  **Commercial Reduction:** During a Jubilee Year (Year 7, 14, 21, etc.), all **Commercial Extraction** permits are automatically reduced by 50% of their base allowance.
2.  **Bottling Ban:** The commercial bottling of water for export is strictly **PROHIBITED** during a Jubilee Year.
3.  **Purpose:** This period is mandated to allow aquifers and surface water levels to recharge naturally.

## Section 3: The Grand Jubilee Reset
1.  **Rights Reset:** During a Grand Jubilee Year (Year 50, 100, etc.), all commercial water usage rights expire and must be re-applied for based on current ecological data.
2.  **Generational Review:** This prevents water monopolies from forming across generations.

## Section 4: Personal Use Rights
1.  **Rainwater Collection:** Every citizen has the absolute right to collect and store rainwater falling on their property. No permit shall ever be required.
2.  **Access:** Access to clean drinking water is a human right. No citizen shall be denied access due to inability to pay.
3.  **Exemption:** Personal Use extraction is exempt from Jubilee reductions.

## Section 5: Pollution & Purity
1.  **Zero Discharge:** It is unlawful to discharge untreated industrial or agricultural waste into any Water Body.
2.  **Penalty:** Violation of this section is a Crime against Purity (Moral Weight: 900), punishable by immediate cessation of operations and heavy fines.

## Section 6: Enforcement & Penalties
1.  **Commercial Violation:** Exceeding extraction limits during a Jubilee year results in a fine equal to 5x the market value of the excess water.
2.  **Restitution:** All fines are paid into the **Water Security Trust**.
