# The Digital Waters Act

## Section 1: Sovereignty over Data
1.  **The 12nm Zone:** The Sovereign Digital Nation claims absolute sovereignty over all data packets originating from or destined to its citizens.
2.  **No Mapping:** Foreign actors are prohibited from mapping the internal network topology of the Nation.

## Section 2: Privacy as Property
1.  **Ownership:** Personal data is the **Private Property** of the citizen.
2.  **Theft:** Unauthorized collection of data is **Grand Theft** (Moral Weight: 500).

## Section 3: Override of UNCLOS
1.  **Innocent Passage:** While UNCLOS allows "Innocent Passage" of ships, we decree that **Data is not a Ship**.
2.  **Inspection:** We reserve the right to inspect any "Foreign Packet" for malicious payloads (Deep Packet Inspection) at the border.
