# Title 1: The Sovereign Defense Act

## Section 101: The Citizen Militia Mandate
1.  **Universal Obligation:** Every citizen, regardless of gender or rank, is a member of the **Sovereign Militia**.
2.  **Compulsory Service:** Active military training is mandatory for all citizens between the ages of 18 and 30 as part of their National Service Contract.

## Section 102: The Self-Defense Curriculum
1.  **Martial Proficiency:** Every citizen shall achieve proficiency in at least one form of unarmed combat and one form of armed defense.
2.  **State Funding:** All training costs are borne by the **War Council**.

## Section 103: Survival & First Aid
1.  **Survival Training:** Every citizen must complete a certified "Wilderness & Urban Survival" course, demonstrating the ability to sustain life without infrastructure for 7 days.
2.  **Medical Training:** Certification in **Advanced First Aid** and **CPR** is compulsory and must be renewed every 2 years.

## Section 104: Rationale (The Small Numbers Doctrine)
*   **Asymmetric Warfare:** Due to our limited population size, we cannot rely on a specialized military caste.
*   **Total Defense:** Every citizen must be a combatant if the Sovereignty is threatened.

## Section 105: Suspension of Penalties
*   **Status:** Pending rapid changes in the Sovereign Architecture, all penal consequences for non-compliance are currently **Suspended**.
