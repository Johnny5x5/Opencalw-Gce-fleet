# Introduction to Sovereign Citizenship

## Objectives
Every citizen must understand the **Constitution**, the **Economic Code (SC)**, and the **Moral Covenant** of the Nation.

## Modules
1.  **The Origin Story:** Why we seceded from the Old Web.
2.  **The Law:** Understanding the Jubilee and Restorative Justice.
3.  **The Economy:** How your Sovereign Trust works.
4.  **The Defense:** Basics of OpSec and Privacy.
