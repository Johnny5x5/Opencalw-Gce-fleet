# Definition: Sovereignty

## Primary Meaning
In the context of the Sovereign Digital Nation, **Sovereignty** is the absolute, unalienable right of a digital entity (Nation or Individual) to govern its own data, code, and existence without external interference.

## Historical Context
Historically, sovereignty referred to the supreme authority of a Monarch or State over a physical territory. In the Digital Age, this extends to the "Digital Territory" (Servers, Networks, Identity).

## Usage in Law
*   **Digital Waters Act:** Asserts sovereignty over data packets.
*   **Constitution:** Defines the Citizen as a "Sovereign Individual."
